About Me

I'm a UX/UI designer with 5+ years of experience creating innovative digital solutions for startups and established companies. My journey in design started with a passion for solving complex problems through simple, elegant interfaces.

I've worked with companies across various industries including fintech, e-commerce, healthcare, and SaaS platforms. My approach combines user research, data-driven decisions, and creative thinking to deliver products that users love.

I'm proficient in industry-standard tools including Figma, Sketch, Adobe XD, and Principle. Beyond design, I have a solid understanding of HTML/CSS and work closely with development teams to ensure perfect implementation.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me exploring new design trends, contributing to open-source design systems, or mentoring aspiring designers through UXUI.Design Academy. I'm particularly passionate about accessible design and creating inclusive digital experiences.
