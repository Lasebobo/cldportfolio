About Me

I'm a multidisciplinary UX/UI designer passionate about transforming complex ideas into intuitive digital experiences. With a background in visual design and human-computer interaction, I bridge the gap between user needs and business goals, creating products that are both beautiful and functional. My process is rooted in empathy, strategy, and user-centric thinking.

Over the years, I've had the privilege of working on diverse projects — from early-stage startups to established tech platforms — across industries like e-commerce, fintech, and social impact. Whether it's crafting mobile apps, designing web platforms, or building design systems, my focus stays on delivering experiences that are intuitive, accessible, and visually compelling. I thrive in a fashion e-commerce experience, and HACKERS, a luxury third-brand platform.

Beyond my client projects, I thrive on collaboration and continuous learning. I believe design is never done — just rather evolves through research, iteration, and real-world feedback. I'm always exploring new tools, techniques, and trends to stay ahead and keep pushing creative boundaries. I'm also an advocate for ethical and inclusive design practices, ensuring that the products I help create serve everyone, not just a select few.
